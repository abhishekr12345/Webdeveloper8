concert

The Winter Carnival NYE 2025 at Crowne Plaza Pune City Centre features a concert-style celebration with live music, DJs, and high-energy performances that create the feel of a full-scale musical event. The entertainment lineup is designed to keep the crowd engaged all night, blending the energy of a concert with the glamour of a New Year’s Eve party. Guests can enjoy live music by Zainul, whose performance adds a soulful and dynamic touch to the evening, alongside DJ Vineet and VDJ Boney, who will spin Bollywood and commercial hits to keep the dance floor alive. The setup includes dazzling décor, professional sound and lighting systems, and a stage that transforms the venue into a vibrant concert arena. Unlike a traditional sit-down show, this concert experience is interactive—guests are encouraged to dance, sing along, and celebrate together as the countdown to midnight approaches. The combination of live vocals, pulsating beats, and theatrical lighting ensures that the Winter Carnival is not just a party but a musical spectacle, giving attendees the thrill of a concert while also offering the indulgence of unlimited food, drinks, and festive ambiance.
