food and drinks

At the Winter Carnival NYE 2025 in Pune, guests can indulge in a lavish spread of food and unlimited drinks, making dining a central highlight of the celebration. The carnival offers a gala dinner by the pool, where master chefs curate an extensive menu of both vegetarian and non-vegetarian delicacies. Guests can enjoy a variety of cuisines at live food counters, ensuring freshly prepared dishes throughout the night. To complement the dining experience, the event includes unlimited spirits and beverages, ranging from cocktails and mocktails to premium selections, so the party atmosphere stays vibrant until midnight and beyond. The combination of gourmet food and free-flowing drinks creates an indulgent experience that matches the carnival’s festive décor and high-energy entertainment. Families are also considered, with options that cater to all age groups, ensuring everyone—from kids to adults—has something delightful to enjoy. Altogether, the food and drinks at the Winter Carnival are designed to elevate the evening into a complete sensory celebration, blending taste, music, and atmosphere into one unforgettable night.
