All entertainment

The Winter Carnival at Crowne Plaza Pune City Centre is designed as a vibrant New Year’s Eve celebration that blends music, dance, dining, and family-friendly fun into one grand night. Guests can expect electrifying performances from DJs like Vineet and VDJ Boney, who will keep the dance floor alive with Bollywood and commercial tracks, alongside live music acts such as Zainul to add variety and energy. The atmosphere is elevated by dazzling décor and festive lighting, creating a glamorous setting for the midnight countdown. Food lovers are treated to a lavish gala dinner by the pool, featuring vegetarian and non-vegetarian delicacies prepared by master chefs, complemented by live food counters and unlimited beverages. Families are also catered to, with a dedicated kids’ zone offering games and activities so parents can enjoy the party while children are entertained in a safe environment. Altogether, the Winter Carnival promises a complete entertainment package—music, dining, and celebration—making it one of Pune’s most exciting ways to welcome the new year.
